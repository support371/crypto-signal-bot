# Crypto Risk Agent Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 11, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the Crypto Risk Agent app (the "App") handles information. The App is intended for paper-only trading simulations and does not engage in real monetary transactions.

## Information We Collect

The App does not collect or store personal data. We do not require user names, email addresses, financial information, or any personally identifiable information to use the App.

## How We Use Information

Since the App does not collect personal data, there is no personal information to use. All data processed through the App pertains to paper-trading activities and system status queries. Trade execution occurs in a simulated environment and does not involve real funds.

## Third-Party Services

The App interacts with third-party services—GitHub, Cloudflare, and Vercel—for the purpose of code management, system deployment, and hosting. These services do not receive any personal data from the App.

## Security

We take security seriously. The App runs with paper-trading configurations only (`TRADING_MODE=paper`; `EXCHANGE_MODE=paper`; `ALLOW_MAINNET=false`). API tokens and secrets are stored securely in platform configuration and are never logged or exposed through the App.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted and reflected by the "Effective Date" above. We encourage users to review the policy periodically.

## Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact the administrator of the Crypto Risk Agent project.
